    David Stein

    Faculty Research Assistant

    Carlson College of Veterinary Medicine, Department of Biomedical Sciences

      David graduated from Oregon State University and has been studying the molecular biology of pathogenic viruses for over 25 years. His major focus has been on developing peptide-conjugated morpholino oligomers (PPMO) for virology research and drug development. He is a huge Beaver fan and has a large collection of cacti!
